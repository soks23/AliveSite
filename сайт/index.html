About the Game

ALIVE isn’t just a moody point-and-click adventure — it’s a whole hand-crafted world inspired by Slavic mythology, full of dark humor and unforgettable characters. Every detail is drawn with care, heart, and a bit of weirdness.

You play as Vi, a 16-year-old girl looking for adventure. What she didn’t expect was to find it this close — one bathroom break away from the car, and suddenly she’s trapped in a strange village where she accidentally brings a dead girl back to life, makes friends with the local crazy grandpa, and gets a witch in serious trouble.

What a way to start a story.

Will Vi manage to untangle this mess and find her way home?

How did Anya die?

Why did the leshy get so mad at Vi?

What are the witches really up to — and what are they hiding?

And where the hell did that talking cat come from?!

All the answers are waiting at the end of the road… but first, you have to take the first step.
